Guided Prayer Time

A reminder that the God of the universe has welcomed you into His presence so that you can talk with Him directly

Confession

Confess your sins to God. Recognize where you've missed the mark

Thanksgiving

Express your gratitude to the God of all the universe who hears your prayers

Supplication

Come to God with your requests and pettitions
